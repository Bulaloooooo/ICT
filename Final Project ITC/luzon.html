Bangui Wind Farm is a wind farm in Bangui, Ilocos Norte, Philippines. The wind farm uses 20 units of 70-meter high Vestas V82 1.65 MW wind turbines,

arranged in a single row stretching along a 9-kilometer shoreline off Bangui Bay, facing the West Philippine Sea.

Binondo

Binondo

For directions: Note: You will be redirect to Google Maps

Binondo is the Chinatown area of Manila. The name comes from the Tagalog word binondoc, which means ‘mountainous’ due to its hilly landscape.

Founded by Spanish Governor Luis Perez Dasmarinas in 1594, it was originally meant for Chinese immigrants who converted to Catholicism, making it the world’s oldest Chinatown district.

It is located at Binondo, Manila

You can contact them at 8-462-8888 or chat thru Viber/Whatsapp at 09993699758

Sagada

Sagada

For directions: Note: You will be redirect to Google Maps

Sagada is a town in the Cordillera Mountains, within the Philippines’ Mountain Province. Echo Valley’s hanging coffins are displayed high on cliffs, while centuries-old coffins are stacked in burial sites like Lumiang Cave.

The nearby Sumaguing Cave has unusual limestone formations. To the north, Bomod-ok Falls has a natural swimming pool. Rice terraces are carved into the area's surrounding mountainsides.

It is located at Province of Mountain Province

You can Globe: 0927 559 2197 & Smart: 0929 521 3229.
