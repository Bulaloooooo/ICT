What to do

What to bring

- Whenever you are going out, prepare a spare just to be sure when going out. Make sure you have spare things to bring.

- Bring some protection tools to protect yourself from danger, like pepper sprays, etc.

- Make sure to bring medicines when planing a vacation outside the country.

What to expect

- Expect that most of the people you are going to, especially when the country does not speak your language, may not understand you so try to be patient

- Expect many people going in the same place you are going.

- Expect the unexpected.

What not to do

-Rely on English to get you by.

-Not Have a Plan B.

-Order Only What You Know.

-Fly Everywhere.

-Not Know How to Get Home.

-Never Have a Cash Stash.
